## What to Look for in a Plex Mini PC

### CPU: Intel QuickSync Is Essential

For Plex transcoding, Intel's integrated graphics are unbeatable. AMD makes great processors but lacks QuickSync.

**Minimum for transcoding:**
- Intel Core i3 12th gen or newer
- Intel Core i5 8th gen or older

**Best options:**
- Intel Core i5 12th/13th gen
- Intel Core i7 11th/12th gen

### RAM: 8GB Minimum, 16GB Better

| Library Size | RAM Needed |
|--------------|------------|
| < 500 items | 8GB |
| 500-2000 items | 16GB |
| 2000-10000 items | 16-32GB |
| 10000+ items | 32GB+ |

RAM is easy to upgrade on most mini PCsâ€”start with what ships and add more later.

### Storage: NVMe + External

Most mini PCs have:
- NVMe SSD slot (for OS and Plex database)
- Potential for 2.5" SATA SSD/HDD

**Recommended setup:**
- 500GB+ NVMe for OS and Plex
- External USB drives or NAS for media storage

### Network: Gigabit Minimum, 2.5GbE Preferred

Your network speed determines:
- How fast you can transfer media to the server
- Maximum bitrate for remote streaming

## Storage Strategy

Mini PCs have limited internal storage. Here's how to handle media:

### Option 1: External Drives

Simple, cheap, but:
- Messy cables
- Drive letters change on reboot sometimes
- No redundancy

WD Elements 12TB - Good value external drive

### Option 2: DAS (Direct Attached Storage)

Multi-bay enclosure connected via USB or Thunderbolt:
- Cleaner than individual drives
- Can do RAID for redundancy
- Expensive

Orico 4-Bay DAS

### Option 3: NAS for Storage, Mini PC for Server

Best of both worlds:
- NAS handles storage with RAID protection
- Mini PC handles transcoding
- Connected via network

**Recommended**: Synology DS224+ [AFFILIATE] for storage, Intel NUC for server.

## Remote Access Considerations

### Plex Relay vs Direct Connect

**Plex Relay** (built-in):
- 1Mbps free, 2Mbps Plex Pass
- Works through any NAT
- Limited quality

**Direct connection**:
- Unrestricted bandwidth
- Requires port forwarding
- Exposes IP

### VPN for Privacy

Use VPN on your streaming devices to:
- Hide your home IP
- Encrypt traffic on public WiFi
- Access region-locked content

NordVPN or ExpressVPN

Or run VPN server on your mini PC for secure remote access.

## FAQ

**Do I need Plex Pass for mini PC transcoding?**
Yes. Hardware transcoding requires Plex Pass ($95 lifetime). Without it, your mini PC can still software transcode, but much less efficiently.

**Can I run other services on my Plex mini PC?**
Yesâ€”this is one advantage over NAS. Run Sonarr, Radarr, Jackett, Home Assistant, Pi-hole, etc. Just ensure enough RAM.

**How much power does a mini PC use?**
Idle: 10-15W (like a night light)
Under load: 40-60W (much less than desktop PC)
At 15W idle: ~$15/year in electricity

**Do mini PCs overheat?**
Modern mini PCs are designed for compact cooling. Ensure good airflow, don't stack things on top, and you'll be fine.

**Can I upgrade parts later?**
Most mini PCs allow RAM and SSD upgrades. CPU and GPU are soldered. Check specs before buying.
